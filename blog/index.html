Director Ellen Pearlman Attends President Biden’s Historic Speech in Poland

As part of her Fulbright Scholar appointment at the University of Warsaw, Thoughtworks Arts Director Ellen Pearlman has been invited to be present in the VIP section of President Biden’s historic speech on February 21st.

The Fulbright Scholar Grant Award is made possible through funds allocated annually by the U.S. Congress and by contributions from partner countries. President Biden’s address to the world will take place at Arkady Kubickiego (gardens of the Royal Castle) in Warsaw.

Director Ellen Pearlman Receives Fulbright Scholar Award

Thoughtworks Arts Director Ellen Pearlman has received the prestigious Fulbright Scholar Award as a Researcher/Professor at the Department of Mathematics at the University of Warsaw, Poland for 2023. As a Fulbrighter, she joins the ranks of many distinguished program participants.

Fulbright scholars represent a wide range of professional and academic disciplines and are competitively selected for the Fulbright program, based on their knowledge, skill sets, and ability to make important contributions to projects overseas.
